garante mais oportunidades inclusive por permitir, de certa forma,
mais variabilidade ou flexibilidade como por exemplo permite mais

Anyway, on the books front, and because market said yes to the 3rd book, 

a soft yes meaning publisher asking me to send the book, 

I prioritized one while in Salamanca,

advanced it, in cold style, less emotion and steady and calm, from 15 pages to around 100-150, so with the expectation to find the world, the goal, ASAP.
